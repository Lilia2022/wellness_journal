About

Overview
A digital platform where individuals can engage in self-care practices, mindfulness, and personal growth through analyzing their mood after they do journaling. Our aim is to create an environment for every user that promotes well-being by motivating users to reflect on their thoughts, emotions and day-to-day experiences, thus enhancing their mental and emotional health.

Our Services
